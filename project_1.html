3-Frame Picture Design

For this specific project, my group and I were tasked with picking abstract art and replicating it in 3D with aframe. After we picked a design, we had some difficulty figuring out the Aframe API. Moving and rotating objects were mostly trial and error, but eventually we were able to succesfully move everything to the right place. The project was a lot of fun and we think it was a good learning experience due to the fact that we had to figure out a system to collaborate effectively.

Code for My Part of the Assignment

For the code, I tried various techniques such as adding different types of objects and working with different perspectives to replicate the image I wanted. However, it was a lot of brute force and just trying varying numbers until the angle was right.
